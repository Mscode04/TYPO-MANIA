TYPO MANIA

Welcome to Typo Mania, a fun and engaging way to improve your typing skills while challenging yourself. This game is designed to make typing enjoyable for everyone, whether you're a beginner or a typing expert.

The game tests your accuracy and speed by allowing you to type your name using a dynamic on-screen keyboard. You can choose from different difficulty levels and compete against time to complete the challenge!

Features

  • Dynamic keyboard layout for an engaging experience.
  • Multiple difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.
  • Interactive and responsive design for all devices.
  • Instant feedback on your performance.
  • Stylish and user-friendly interface.
