Welcome to Typo Mania Documentation

Your guide to mastering the art of chaotic typing!

1. What is Typo Mania?

Typo Mania is not just a game; it’s a battlefield where your typing skills are tested under extreme (and funny) conditions. Imagine trying to type your name on a keyboard designed by a hyperactive cat. Yes, it's that exciting.

2. How to Play?

  • Step 1: Enter your name (or any name—be creative, like "Mr. Typo Genius").
  • Step 2: Select a difficulty level. Each level adjusts the speed of the keyboard refresh:
    • Easy : The keyboard changes every 1.5 seconds, giving you ample time to type.
    • Medium : The keyboard changes every 1.2 seconds, challenging your typing reflexes.
    • Hard : The keyboard changes every 1 second—test your skills under pressure!
  • Step 3: Hit the "Start Game" button and prepare for the chaos.
  • Step 4: Type your name using the virtual keyboard before time runs out. (Tip: No, shouting at the screen doesn't help.)

3. Features You’ll Love

  • Dynamic keyboard layout that changes every time. (Good luck!)
  • Multiple difficulty levels for beginners, pros, and daredevils.
  • Instant feedback to make you laugh (or cry).
  • Fully responsive design—play on your phone, tablet, or even your fridge screen (if it supports it).

4. Pro Tips

  • Stay calm. Panicking only makes it worse.
  • Memorize your name (seriously, it helps).
  • Have a cup of coffee before starting. Typing is better with caffeine.
